Who I am

I'm 29 years old and a self-taught developer. What started with some small automation tasks at work grew to a passion that I'm looking to turn into a full-time job. I've been coding in Python and VBA for a little over a year and currently working on a few front-end projects.


When I'm not coding you might catch me out on the tennis court, jogging around Central Park, or down at Barcade in the village.

Languages

I have experience with automation tasks using Python and VBA including libraries such as Selenium for web scraping, win32com for email automation, and xlswriter for aggregating data from python into excel, etc. Over the past year I reduced over 150 hours of manual work through scripting.


I have been learning HMTL, CSS and Javacript since October of 2019 and continue to strive learn more each day.
